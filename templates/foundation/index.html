About Us

Help Me Climb Foundation was formed in 2014 by Mr Shreenavas Potnis and Varsha Potnis.

In 2019 Help Me Climb Foundation was passed to Dr. Mangesh Kadve and his team to take care in field of helthcare education and services.

At Help Me Climb Foundation we are focused on providing high quality primary care in low cost to the poor and underprivileged communites. Our healthcare tieup centres with General medicine,Heart care neurosurgery dignostic services make medical care accessible,affordable and available to the rural and urban under-served communities across the state.

We provide our patients with medical consultation we also provide subsidized heart care neurosurgery in collaboration with various hospital and doctors and diagnostic services.

Till date we have served over 4000 patients at our helthcare centers and our mission is to improve the lives of many more.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Section 80G of income Tax act?

    The deductions under the Section 80G are the contributions made to charitable organisations or institutions with 80G exemption certificate. However, not all donations are eligible under this section.

  • What Tax exemption benefit do I get?

    As per the revised tax exemption act, effective April 1, 2017, donations above INR 500 to Help Me Climb Foundation will be eligible for 50% tax exemption under Section 80G of Income Tax Act.

  • What is the minimum amount that needs to be donated to get a tax exemption?

    A minimum of INR 500 should be donated to avail tax exemption under IT Sec 80G.

  • What if the Tax receipt is misplaced?

    We may not be able to provide you a duplicate receipt. But we can send you the Income Tax Exemption certificate copy.

  • How can you donate to HMCF?

    You can donate through HMCF Website, Cheque, Paytm QR, Online Transfer (IMPS, UPI, NEFT & RTGS)

  • Is there tax exemptions on cash donations as well?

    Cash donations above INR 500 are applicable for 80G certificates.
